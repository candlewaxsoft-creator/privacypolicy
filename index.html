Privacy Policy for Candlewax

Last Updated: February 12, 2026

1. Overview

Candlewax ("the extension") is a productivity utility designed to help users archive their professional conversations by locally extracting transcripts and summaries from the Gong platform. This policy outlines our commitment to data minimization and user privacy.

2. Information Collection and Disclosure

To fulfill its single purpose, the extension processes the following categories of user data:

  • Personal Communications: The extension extracts call transcripts, participant names, and conversation metadata from your Gong account.
  • Website Content: The extension parses text, summaries, and structural UI elements directly from app.gong.io to generate exportable files.

3. Local Processing & Storage

Your privacy is protected by the technical architecture of Candlewax:

  • Zero Data Transmission: All data extraction and file formatting occur locally within your browser. No transcripts or personal information are ever transmitted to external servers or third parties.
  • No Persistent Storage: We do not maintain a database of your conversations. Data is cleared from the extension's temporary memory once the local file generation is complete.
  • No Analytics: We do not track your usage, clicks, or web history.

4. Permissions Justification

The extension requires the following permissions to function:

  • scripting & host_permissions: To parse transcript data from the Gong interface.
  • downloads: To save the generated TXT and CSV files to your device.
  • alarms & tabs: To manage the background processes and tabs required for bulk downloads.

5. Compliance and Limited Use

This extension complies with the Chrome Web Store User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. We do not sell, trade, or rent user data to any third party.

For questions regarding this policy, please contact the developer through the Chrome Web Store support link.
