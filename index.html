Privacy Policy for Candlewax

Last Updated: February 12, 2026

1. Overview

Candlewax ("the extension") is a productivity utility designed to help users download their own call transcripts and summaries from a call recording platform. We follow a "Privacy by Design" approach, ensuring that your data remains under your local control.

2. Information Collection and Use

The extension extracts call transcripts, participant names, dates, and summaries directly from the app.gong.io pages you visit. This data is used for the sole purpose of generating local files (TXT and CSV) for your personal use.

3. Local Processing

  • No Remote Transmission: All data extraction and file formatting occur locally within your browser.
  • No Data Storage: We do not store your transcripts on any external database or server.
  • Zero Tracking: We do not use cookies or analytics to monitor your activity.

4. Permissions Disclosure

To function properly, Candlewax requires the following permissions:

  • scripting: To read transcript text from the Gong interface.
  • downloads: To save generated files to your device.
  • storage: To maintain session status during bulk operations.

5. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transmit your data to third parties. Because data is not collected on our servers, it cannot be shared with advertisers or data brokers.

This policy is compliant with the Chrome Web Store User Data Policy.
